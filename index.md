# Privacy Policy for Bunny Beats

_Last updated: 2026 Feb 3_

Studio Tapioca, LLC does not collect, store, or process personal data from players of **Bunny Beats**, except as described below.

---

## Steam Leaderboards

The game uses Steam’s built-in leaderboard functionality to display scores. Any information shown on leaderboards (such as Steam usernames and scores) is collected and managed by Valve Corporation in accordance with Steam’s Privacy Policy.

Studio Tapioca does not have access to personal Steam account data.

---

## Optional Feedback Form

Bunny Beats includes an **optional feedback form** that players may choose to submit. When using this form, you may voluntarily provide information such as:

- Written feedback or comments  
- Bug reports  
- Contact information (such as an email address), if you choose to include it  

This information is used solely for the purpose of reviewing feedback and improving the game. Submission of personal information is optional, and no information is required to play the game.

We do not use feedback data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or marketing, and we do not sell or share this information with third parties.

---

## No Additional Data Collection

Aside from the optional feedback form and Steam-provided services, Bunny Beats does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising systems.

---

## Third-Party Services

Steam services are provided by Valve Corporation. For information on how Steam handles your data, please review their Privacy Policy:

[https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/](https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/)
